Tune in every other Thursday at 17:00 UTC / 12:00 ET, and watch previous editions of [DevNation: The Show on-demand](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLf3vm0UK6HKpbZvVaYjOmnvnVXBkHPvms)!
